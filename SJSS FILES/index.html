Admission

Admission to the school is based on academic merit.The school is a boys' school and therefore admits boys from all corners of the country.

The only special provision apart from academic merit is that a student to be admitted should be a Catholic. This is because the school is a minor seminary.It not only trains students for excellence but also forms students having aspirations of becoming priests.

Intake is ongoing for:

  • Grade 8
  • Form 1
  • Form 2
  • Form 3
  • Form 4
