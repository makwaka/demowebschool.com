St. Joseph junior seminary is a Catholic boarding school.It is locatd 4KM from Molo town The institution is located in a serene environment; conducive for learning.
We offer a holistic growth of students by not embracing a culture of perfomance but also that of self-growth.Our students are engaged both in class activities but also in out-of-class activities.
There are clubs in school that help to improve the confidence and sel-fulfillment of students. This clubs are:

  1. Drama club
  2. Scouts' club
  3. Music club
  4. Young farmers' club
  5. Environmntal club
  6. Fitness club
  7. St. John's Ambulance

The teachers act as patrons of the above clubs while learners are tasked with coming up with projects in a self-directed manner.
The students engage in debates/mijadala every once a week tackling issues affecting them and also national issues.This greatily improves their expression; sharpening their grammar and public-speaking skills.
The school is headed by two priests- the Rector and the vice-Rector who doubles as a spiritual director.
The students are also involved in the school administration through a strong and vibrant prefects' body who are tasked with channeling the grievances of the students.
